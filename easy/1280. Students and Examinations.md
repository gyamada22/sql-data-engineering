# 1280. Students and Examinations

**Data:** 11/12/2025  
**Dificuldade:** Easy  
**Tópicos:** SQL, CROSS JOIN, Subquery, ISNULL, Aggregation  
**Link:** [Students and Examinations - LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/problems/students-and-examinations/description/)

## Descrição
Escreva uma solução para encontrar o número de vezes que cada aluno participou de cada exame. Retorne o resultado ordenado por student_id e subject_name.
Primeira vez usando crossjoin
## Solução (SQL Server)

```sql
SELECT 
    s.student_id,
    s.student_name,
    sub.subject_name,
    ISNULL((
        SELECT COUNT(*) 
        FROM Examinations e
        WHERE e.student_id = s.student_id 
            AND e.subject_name = sub.subject_name
    ), 0) AS attended_exams
FROM Students s
CROSS JOIN Subjects sub
ORDER BY s.student_id, sub.subject_name
